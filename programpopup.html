Description Start by lying on your back with your knees bent, feet flat on the floor and your arms resting by your sides with your palms facing down. Engage your core as prescribed. Keeping your shoulders down, slowly extend your hips up, aiming to form a straight line from your knees to your shoulders and then lower yourself down to complete the exercise. Remember to keep your head and shoulders on the ground throughout the exercise. Image Video Add to Program
